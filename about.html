A Few Words About Me

I am a fourth year undergraduate marketing student at The University of Northern Colorado. I am pursuing a B.S. in Business Administration and a minor in Digital Marketing. I have a career focus in Digital Marketing, with a strong passion for website optimization. I enjoy hanging out with my friends & family, cooking, and listening to music.

Career Interests

My current career interests are focused around digital marketing. Other areas of marketing and digital marketing that I would like to explorer further in my career are social media marketing, market research, and UI & UX design. I am also excited to be starting a digital marketing internship with a holstic sports recovery business in Denver, CO this coming January.

Activities & Hobbies

Aside from being a full time student, I am a part-time business tutor for Tutorial Services here at UNC, and an active member of my sorority, Sigma Kappa. I have always had a passion for helping others being a part of something bigger than myself. Both of these activities have given me opportunities to be an contributive individual in my community and grow within my leadership. In my sorority, I have held 3 different leadership positions: Public Relations Chairwoman, Vice President of Programming, and Social Chairwoman. In these roles I have gained many technical and transferrable skills. I like to spend my free time skiing, cooking, and focusing on personal wellness.

Goals

My ultimate goal in life is to be happy. I think the major components that contribute to my goal of happiness are my career, family, and community. I think haveing a happy balance between all aspects of life is the best way to achieve happiness; this balance looks different for everyone. I personally get satisfaction out of hard work and making a career. This is why it is my goal to find a job that I am so passionate about, it doesn't feel like work at all. I want to be close to my family and support them as much as I can where they may need it. I also want to build a community whther that be based around one of my hobbies, or my nextdoor neighbors; they say it takes a village- and it really does.
